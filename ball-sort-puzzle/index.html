What Is Ball Sort Puzzle?

Ball Sort Puzzle is a simple logic game where each tube holds a stack of colored balls. Your goal is to move the balls until every tube contains only one color.

How to Play Ball Sort Puzzle Online

Select a tube to pick the top ball, then select another tube. You can move a ball into an empty tube or onto a ball with the same color.

Tips to Solve Ball Sort Puzzle

Keep at least one tube empty when possible. Avoid burying a color under too many different balls, and use empty tubes to expose the colors you need.

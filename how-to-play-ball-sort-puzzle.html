Rules and strategy

How to Play Ball Sort Puzzle

Ball Sort Puzzle is easy to learn: move balls between tubes until each tube contains only one color.

Basic Rules

  • Only the top ball in a tube can move.
  • You can move a ball into an empty tube.
  • You can move a ball onto another ball of the same color.
  • You cannot move a ball into a full tube.
  • You win when every non-empty tube has one color only.

Beginner Tips

Keep one tube empty as long as possible. Choose one color to solve first, and avoid filling every tube too early. If a move does not reveal a useful color, consider another route.

Common Mistakes

  • Using both empty tubes too early.
  • Covering a nearly solved tube with a different color.
  • Moving randomly without exposing blocked colors.
